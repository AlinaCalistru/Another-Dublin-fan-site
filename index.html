In love with Dublin

Resons to fall in love with Dublin

Dublin was listed by the Globalization and World Cities Research Network as a global city, with ranking of "Alpha-", which places it amongst the top thirty cities in the world. It is a historical and contemporary centre for education, the arts, administration, economy and industry.

  • Splendid architecture with colourful doors.
  • Magnificent castles. Malahide is magic.
  • Everything you need at your doorstep.
  • Great events.
  • Wonderful parks and gardens.
  • Fantastic playgrounds.
  • Great public transport.
  • City of James Joyce.
  • The coastline. Howth is so hospitable.
  • Rainbows like nowhere.
  • Delicious food.
  • Formidable leprechaun stories.
